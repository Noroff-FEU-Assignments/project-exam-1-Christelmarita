About Us

Who are we?

At SCANDI GAZETTE, we are a team of three passionate women who are dedicated to bringing you the best of Scandinavia, including its captivating design. Together, we form a dynamic team, combining our love for Scandinavian design, culture, and cuisine.

Our collaboration allows us to curate a collection of captivating articles, stunning visuals, and engaging content that celebrates the essence of Scandinavian design. Join us on this journey as we invite you to discover the beauty and innovation of Scandinavian design through our eyes. Let us introduce ourselves and share our unique perspectives:

“Our blog’s mission is to make Scandinavian culture, design, and lifestyle accessible to all. Explore captivating articles, visuals, and anecdotes as we celebrate the wonders of this part of the world together!” - Sofia

First, there's Anna, our intrepid explorer and design enthusiast. With an unwavering love for Scandinavian aesthetics, she traverses the region, uncovering hidden design gems and architectural marvels. Anna's keen eye for detail and her appreciation for minimalist beauty inspire us to showcase the timeless elegance and functionality of Scandinavian design.

Next, we have Emma, our cultural connoisseur and history buff. Emma delves into the rich heritage of Scandinavian design, exploring its roots and evolution over time. Her in-depth research and knowledge enable us to provide you with engaging articles that illuminate the stories behind iconic Scandinavian designers and their groundbreaking creations.

Lastly, there's Sofia, our culinary expert with a passion for design. Sofia believes that Scandinavian design extends beyond furniture and decor—it's also embodied in the presentation and aesthetics of Nordic cuisine. Through her culinary explorations, Sofia uncovers the artistry and attention to detail that make Scandinavian dining experiences truly exceptional.
